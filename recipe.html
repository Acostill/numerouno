Chopped Cheese Sandwhich

Ingredients

  • tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 1/4 pounds ground beef, broken into pieces
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 to 4 tablespoons chopped pickled hot peppers
  • 8 slices American cheese (about 4 ounces)
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 4 soft white hero rolls, split and toasted
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce, sliced tomatoes, ketchup and hot sauce, for topping

Directions

  1. Heat a large cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the vegetable oil, then add the onion,
    ground beef, 1/2 teaspoon salt and a few grinds of pepper.
  3. Cook, undisturbed, until the beef starts to develop a crust, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Flip with a spatula, breaking up the meat.
  5. Continue cooking until browned, about 30 more seconds.
  6. Stir in the pickled peppers, then arrange the cheese over the meat mixture;
    remove from the heat and let melt.
  7. Spread the mayonnaise on both sides of the rolls.
  8. Divide the beef among the roll bottoms using a spatula.
  9. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, ketchup, hot sauce and the roll tops.
